WMARM-like caveat
=================
The original APHREH-ADSMap WMARM is a population-weighted spatial aggregation
across multiple Basic Spatial Areas (BSAs). This dataset contains a single
city-level BSA (Milan as one unit), so population-weighted spatial aggregation
is mathematically trivial. The score reported here is therefore a WMARM-LIKE
relevance score, computed as the inverse-CI-weighted standardized effect
(se = Vul / sd_r) summarized across years for each (pollutant, outcome, lag),
and is not a full spatial WMARM.
